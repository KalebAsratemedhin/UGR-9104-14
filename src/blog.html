Ethiopia-Hack Hackathon

Nov 7, 2018

For all Ethiopian tech enthusiasts and programmers, GDG Addis has organized 12 consecutive Hackathon programs in Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, Jimma and Dire Dawa funded by U.S. Embassy in Ethiopia and USAID. "Ethiopia Hack" v1.0 will take place from Nov 16 to 18 , 2018 at American Center. It is themed "Build for digital wellbeing". In each hackathon program, 10 teams (each with 5 members) will compete to design, build, collaborate and win. Winners ranked from 1st to 3rd will be awarded big prizes. Interested people can register here.

You can register as an individual or as a team of 5 members. For Individuals, we will let to form a team one week a head of the hackathon. We target participants with age between 18 to 25. Women are highly appreciated to participate. Minimum coding experience is required. There will be a one-day pre-hack training before 1 week a head of each hackathon program.
